Looking Ahead

Smart Traffic is about more than just resolving the gridlock problems of today, it’s about preparing for a future of self-driving cars powered by 5G networks. For the public sector, Smart Traffic is a chance to gain valuable data, experience, and comfort with Edge Computing, AI, IoT, and a variety of other technologies that the autonomous vehicles of the future will depend on. For Telecoms, it’s a chance to start laying down the relevant infrastructural foundation now, which will make their future 5G depolyment work faster and easier, while generating some reliable long-tail revenue.
